## **💻 About Me**

My name is Charles Johnson; I’m an open-source software developer and a full-time student. I’m currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree at Tennessee Tech as a computer science major with an emphasis in cybersecurity and a concentration in medical information assurance.

Whenever I’m not studying or worshiping at my local parish, I enjoy developing all kinds of utilities, as well as finding bugs & vulnerabilities in applications developed by companies and independent organizations.

While I primarily develop desktop and web applications, I also create high-quality CLI applications.

I have experience with a variety of programming/markup languages, such as: HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, Python, C#, C++, Bash, and PowerShell.

Outside of my technical experience, I am a Creative Designer. My background spans beyond programming, I have a vast amount of experience in Graphic Design, and Video Editing.
